Eduardo Vázquez Carvajal

Eduardo Vázquez Carvajal (Mexico, 1998) is currently working on a degree in Art for

Videogames in COCO School Mexico (COCO). He deeply believes in the human’s

potential: humans are capable of transforming and evolving through experiences, good or

not, gaining inside to everyday taboos and dismissed topics and reaching higher mental

awareness about the world that surrounds us. He finds interest in human nature,

philosophy, art, writing, design, psychology and narrative. He is interested in working in all

types of professional environments and multidisciplinary teams, aspiring to become a

lead artist and writer within the game industry. He currently focuses on writing, aesthetic

design of games and entertainment media. He has been in charge of designing a special

cover illustration for a scholar magazine. He has participated in the 2019 Game Jam,

creating the game “The Bright Unknown”, being responsible for the aesthetic design and

art direction. He has taken a CAE certification with a final grade of B. He is also well-

informed and trained in the Scrum and Trello method of management for product

development.
